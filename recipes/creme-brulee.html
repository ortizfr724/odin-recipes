Caramel Crème Brûlée

To conclude your culinary journey, indulge in the timeless elegance of a Caramel Crème Brûlée. This decadent dessert, with its crisp sugar crust and silky smooth custard, is the perfect finale to any meal.Pair it with a classic Italian Espresso enhanced with a zesty lemon twist, to awaken your senses and leave you feeling satisfied. this delightful combination is a true testament to the art of dessert, offering a blend of sweet, creamy, and bitter flavors that will leave you craving more.

Ingredients

For the Custard

  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 large egg yokes
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Caramel

  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

Equipment

  • 2 small oven safe ramekins
  • Kitchen torch

Instructions

Make the Caramel

  1. Heat a small saucepan over medium heat. Add the granulated sugar and cook, swirling the pan occasionally, until the sugar melts and turns a deep amber color.
  2. Carefully pour the caramel into the bottom of the ramekins, swirling to coat the bottom.Carefully pour the caramel into the bottom of the ramekins, swirling to coat the bottom.

Prepare the Custard

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream and milk over medium heat until just simmering.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and sugar until pale and smooth.
  3. Slowly whisk the hot cream mixture into the egg yolks, then whisk in the vanilla extract.
  4. Strain the custard mixture into the ramekins, making sure to cover the caramel.

Bake

  1. Place the ramekins in a water bath in a baking dish. Pour hot water into the baking dish, ensuring the water reaches halfway up the sides of the ramekins.
  2. Bake in a preheated 325°F (165°C) oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the custard is set but still slightly jiggly in the center.
  3. Remove from the oven and let cool completely.

caramelized the Top

  1. Sprinkle a thin layer of granulated sugar over the top of each custard.
  2. Use a kitchen torch to caramelize the sugar until golden brown.
