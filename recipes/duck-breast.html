Pan Seared Duck Breast

As the days grow shorter and the air turns crisp, there's no better way to indulge in the flavors of the season than with a Pan Seared Duck Breast. This rich and savory dish, paired with creamy mashed potatoes and a sweet-tart red cherry glaze, is the epitome of autumnal comfort food. To complement the robust flavors of the duck, a glass of full-bodied Shiraz offers a perfect pairing, with its dark fruit notes and velvety tannins enhancing the overall dining experience.
